Social Metrics

Influencer campaign performance — auto-updated daily

Data Collection

Metrics are scraped automatically once per day using yt-dlp, an open-source media tool. The scraper reads every Google Sheet shared with the service account, finds rows with video URLs, and pulls the latest stats (views, likes, comments, shares) directly from each platform. Updated metrics are written back to the sheet automatically.

Platform Support

TikTok — Fully supported. Public video metrics are available without authentication. This is the primary platform for most campaigns.

YouTube — Fully supported. Public video metrics are available without authentication.

Instagram — Limited. Meta requires authentication to access Reel metrics programmatically. The scraper will attempt the first Instagram URL in each tab — if it fails (which is expected), it automatically skips the remaining Instagram URLs to save time. Instagram metrics may need to be entered manually.

Adding a New Campaign

Share your Google Sheet with the service account email shown below (Editor access). It will be discovered automatically on the next page load. To populate metrics immediately, click "Update Metrics Now" (if available) or wait for the next daily run.

Column Compatibility

The system auto-detects your column layout. Your URL column can be named "Link", "Post", or "URL". Country can be "Country" or "Territory". Content type can be "Vertical" or "Creative". As long as the sheet has a URL column and a Views column, it will be detected.
