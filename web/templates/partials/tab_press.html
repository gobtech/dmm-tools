• Powered by GANGSIGNS — a 7-source pipeline: RSS/WordPress feeds, XML sitemaps, Google News, SearXNG, Serper, Tavily, and DuckDuckGo.
  • Results are matched against our 1,500+ outlet database, grouped by country. Social media posts are classified via a handle registry (482 IG, 486 FB, 429 X handles).
  • All results are date-verified — dates are extracted from RSS, HTML meta tags, JSON-LD, and URL patterns. Undated articles are resolved by fetching the page directly.
  • Custom date range enforces strict start/end bounds with date evidence required for every result.
  • Queries are enriched with release schedule data (album/single titles) for smarter, more targeted searches.
  • AI relevance filtering via Groq removes false positives. Article titles shown as hyperlinks in reports.
  • Typically completes in ~2 minutes. Source breakdown shown in report footer.

Reports will be appended to linked Google Docs. Artists without linked docs will be skipped.
